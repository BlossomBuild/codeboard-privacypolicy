CodeBoard is a simple and private macOS app built with SwiftUI. Your privacy is important, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Overview

CodeBoard helps you turn code snippets and images into shareable designs. The app works completely on your device and does not collect any personal information.

Data Collection

CodeBoard does not collect, store, or share any user data.
There are no analytics services, tracking tools, or third-party data collectors integrated into the app.

Local Storage

All information you create inside CodeBoard is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.
Nothing is uploaded to any server.
You have full control over your data at all times.

Account and Login

CodeBoard does not require an account.
You can use the app without signing in or providing any personal details.

Internet Usage

The app does not send or receive any data over the internet.
Any network access is only for downloading app updates through the App Store itself.

Third-Party Services

CodeBoard does not use any third-party services that collect information.
